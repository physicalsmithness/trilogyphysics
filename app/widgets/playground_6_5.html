      Param cheat-sheet (the customisable surface)
      
    
        
  • Common: axes:{xmax,xstep,ymax,ystep,minorDiv,xlabel,ylabel} (units go in the label strings; minorDiv = little squares per big box); mark:{...} shows/hides annotations everywhere. Axes overrides are PARTIAL: give only the fields you want changed (e.g. {"axes":{"ymax":20}}) and the rest keep the kind's defaults. This works on presets and scenarios too, and on the vector grids.
    • 
        
  • Journey phases (motion_graph, area_under_vt): phases:[{dur,v0,v1,shape}], shape = linear | hold | curve_up | curve_down | asymptote | over_top | under_bottom.
    • 
        
  • motion_graph: kind = distance_time | displacement_time | speed_time | velocity_time | acceleration_time; scenario presets walk_to_shops / bus_journey; variant:"curved_constant" distractor.
    • 
        
  • area_under_vt: display = squares | regions | none; from,to; mark: squareCaption, regionLabels, areaValues.
    • 
        
  • gradient_tool: curve:"line"|"curve" or fspec:{type:"power"|"linear"|"asymptote",...} + domain + axes; tangentAt, readPoints, chordFrom, variant:"chord"; mark: triangle, deltas, working, label, dropLines.
    • 
        
  • braking_vt: condition or speed/reaction_time/decel; compare:[{condition,label,dash}] + keyLabel; variants rising_reaction | sloped_reaction | curved_brake; mark: areas, values, caption, dropLines, key.
    • 
        
  • stopping_distance_speed: reaction_time, decel, vmax, show:[...], variants thinking_quadratic | braking_linear.
    • 
        
  • free_body_diagram: object = box | ball | trolley | cart | car | car_trailer | boat | person; facing:"left"|"right" turns directional sprites around; preset or forces:[{name,dir,mag,showMag}] (dir = up/down/left/right or degrees).
    • 
        
  • ramp_fbd: angle, object, friction, resolve, variants weight_along_slope | normal_vertical.
    • 
        
  • vector_addition: vectors:[{mag,angle}], quantity, unit, method scale|maths, unitsPerBox, variant:"distance_scalar"; mark: resultant, scaleCaption, protractor, vectorLabels, working.
    • 
        
  • vector_resolve: mag, angle, method calculation|triples|scale, variant:"sin_cos_swapped"; mark: components, working, angle, rightAngle.
    • 
        
  • spring_extension: arrangement single|series|parallel, masses, naturalLen, extPerMass, variant:"total_as_extension".
    • 
        
  • collision_illustration: type sticky|bouncy|explosion, m1,m2,u1,u2,v2, object1/object2 (sprites), facing1/facing2 (default: bodies face along their motion; at rest they face the other body), showValues.
    • 
        
  • Interactive vector_addition = the full staged drawing flow (choose scale → drag the green tip to draw each line → ruler → convert → protractor → reference). vector_addition_quick is the plain numeric form.
    • 
        
  • Interactive configs also take: marks, tolerance, toleranceSquares, slopeTolerance, minRun, angleTolerance, legTolerance, measureTolerance, scaleChoices, targetT/tangentAt.
